      IOI coin,
      is a stablecoin designed to maintain the stability of its value by following the value of a stable underlying asset.
      The aim is to allow users to transact with cryptocurrencies without having to worry about the high fluctuations in value
      that are common with volatile cryptocurrencies.
      

      IOI coin,
      the middle price is taken from the top 5 stablecoins on the market, this is done in order to provide reliable price stability for users.
      In this case, "mid price" refers to the average price of the five major stablecoins consisting of A coin ($A),
      B coin ($B), C coin ($C), D coin ($D), and E coin ($E). In this way, IOI coin ensures that its value is not too far from the market
      prices of other stablecoins, which makes it easily accepted and used by cryptocurrency users around the world.

* cBc (Crypto Bank Chain). - read also - How it Work

        cBc (Crypto Bank Chain) is a decentralized bank that operates on a blockchain system.
        The blockchain system used by cBc is a distributed network of computers that are connected and
        communicate with each other through a special protocol called the blockchain.
      

        In the blockchain system, every transaction and data that enters the network will be recorded in the form of blocks
        that are connected to each other and encrypted using strong cryptographic algorithms.
        The blocks are then stored in the nodes on the network,
        and each node has a copy of all the blocks on the network.
      

        cBc uses blockchain technology to create a decentralized bank that is not dependent on a single central entity or authority.
        In the cBc system, every transaction and incoming data will be recorded and verified by all nodes in the network,
        so that no one party has full control over the system.
      

        cBc also uses strong cryptography to secure every transaction and data that enters the network.
        Every transaction that occurs on the network will be encoded using a strong cryptographic algorithm,
        so that it is not easily hacked or manipulated by unauthorized parties.
      

        In addition, cBc also uses a special token called $ioi digital currency to facilitate transactions on the network.
        $ioi can be used to pay transaction fees or as a medium of exchange within the network, and has a stable value.
      

        In all, cBc is a decentralized bank that operates on a secure, transparent blockchain system,
        and does not rely on a single central entity or authority.
        In the cBc system, every transaction and data will be recorded and verified by all nodes on the network,
        and protected by strong cryptography.
